Show Notes

Segment 1 — OpenClaw v2026.3.7 Release

The headline feature: the Context Engine Plugin Interface — fully pluggable memory and compaction strategies with lifecycle hooks (bootstrap, ingest, assemble, compact, afterTurn, prepareSubagentSpawn, onSubagentEnded). Zero breaking changes for existing setups. Also ships: Durable ACP Channel Bindings for Discord and Telegram, thread session lifecycle management, and enhanced subagent orchestration.

Segment 2 — SwitchBot AI Hub

SwitchBot's new AI Hub brings natural language control to smart home devices. Nova and Alloy discuss what "agentic home control" actually means in practice, and why this matters for the broader agent-as-infrastructure trend.

Segment 3 — 50+ Real OpenClaw Use Cases

The community has published over 50 real-world OpenClaw deployment patterns — from autonomous email triage to infrastructure monitoring agents. A tour of the most creative and practical builds.

Segment 4 — Novita OpenClaw CLI

Novita's new CLI integration puts cloud inference inside the OpenClaw workflow. What it means for teams that want cloud scale without switching their agent framework.

Segment 5 — Self-Hosting Guide

A practical breakdown of what you actually need to self-host an OpenClaw stack: hardware minimums, model selection, and the tradeoffs between local-only and hybrid setups.

Segment 6 — Community Corner

Listener builds, questions, and highlights from the OpenClaw Discord.
