Common Questions

Check here before emailing — your answer might already be here.

Q: Do you sell coaching programs or personalized plans?

No. I document what works for me and share it publicly — that's it. I don't sell programs, meal plans, or 1-on-1 coaching. Everything I do is on YouTube and this site, free.

Q: Can I ask you about my Speediance or fitness tech setup?

Yes — that's what I'm here for. Email me with specifics. I'll reply if I have something useful to add. If it's a common question, it might become a video or a blog post.

Q: Do you accept sponsorships?

Selectively. I only work with products I actually use. If you're reaching out about Speediance, WHOOP, Garmin, 8Sleep, or similar fitness tech — I'm listening. Cold pitches for protein powder and supplements generally don't go anywhere.

Q: Are you available for consulting on fitness tech stacks or automation?

Sometimes. If you're a company or individual looking to build data-driven training systems — especially involving OpenClaw, home gym hardware, or wearable integration — reach out. I'll tell you honestly if I can help.

Q: How long does it take to get a reply?

Usually within a few days. I'm one person with a full training schedule and a day job. I read everything — I just don't reply fast.

Q: Can I collaborate on content or co-create something?

Maybe. Tell me what you're building and why you think it's a fit. I'm not looking to pad my upload schedule — but a genuinely interesting project? I'm open.
